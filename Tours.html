Experience the best of Boston with CityPASS, an easy-to-use ticket booklet that gives you admission to museums all at a great savings. BOSTON NIGHTLIFE TOUR
The Boston Night Tour is a great (and macabre) way to explore the Hub. Compared to Ghosts and Gravestones, I would recommend this tour.
Our tour guides, Caitlin and Andrew, are great storytellers who give you a sense of history, context, and possible scientific explanations for supernatural occurrences reported in the area. They talk with you as opposed to dressing up as phony characters and "performing" in front of you (as found on the Ghosts and Gravestones Tour). Highlights included: tales of Charles Dickens, spirit photography, and murderous hotel rooms. You may even have a supernatural occurrence at the Great Elm though the Scully in me wonders if it was legit.

BOSTON HISTORIC TOUR
Welcome to the Freedom Trail, a 2.5-mile, red-lined route that leads you to 16 historically significant sites — each one an authentic treasure. Explore museums and meetinghouses, churches, and burying grounds. Learn about the brave people who shaped our nation. Discover the rich history of the American Revolution, as it began in Boston, where every step tells a story. The Freedom Trail Foundation offers a daily schedule of public walking tours featuring several sites along the Freedom Trail.
